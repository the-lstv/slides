Welcome to slides.lstv.space

Here I host various presentations I made mostly for school with my own presentation framework. It's quite simple thanks to my backend and frontend frameworks (Akeno and LS). Each presentation is just a simple HTML file that includes the template.
It directly supports *markdown* and animations & transitions are basically free. It's probably the most flexible way to make presentations if you are a developer :)
